# 1. Purpose

InfraTrack is an enterprise Asset and Field Operations Management Platform designed for Australian Local Governments.

The platform supports the complete operational lifecycle of public infrastructure assets by centralising inspections, maintenance activities, work requests, contractor interventions, documentation, approvals and historical records.

Its primary objective is to provide councils with a single source of operational truth for managing field activities while improving traceability, accountability and decision making.

---

# 2. Vision

Public infrastructure represents one of the largest long-term investments made by local governments.

Maintaining these assets requires continuous inspections, maintenance planning, field operations and administrative validation.

InfraTrack aims to provide a unified platform allowing managers, field employees and contractors to collaborate throughout the complete lifecycle of every public asset.

Every inspection, decision, maintenance activity and validation becomes part of the permanent history of the asset.

---

# 3. Business Problem

Many small and medium-sized councils still rely on multiple disconnected systems such as spreadsheets, emails, shared folders, PDF reports and paper inspection forms.

As a consequence:

* asset information becomes fragmented;
* inspections are difficult to track;
* historical maintenance records are incomplete;
* supporting evidence is scattered across multiple systems;
* reporting is time-consuming;
* operational visibility is limited;
* accountability becomes difficult during audits or public enquiries.

Field staff often collect valuable information that is never fully integrated into the organisation's long-term asset history.

---

# 4. Proposed Solution

InfraTrack centralises the complete operational workflow into a single platform.

The platform manages:

* public assets;
* Operational activities initiated by business events;
* field inspections;
* identified issues;
* work orders;
* maintenance activities;
* contractor interventions;
* approvals;
* supporting documents;
* photographs;
* operational history.

Rather than focusing solely on maintenance, InfraTrack preserves the complete operational evidence associated with every asset throughout its lifecycle.

---

# 5. Objectives

The primary objectives of InfraTrack are to:

* centralise operational asset information;
* simplify field inspections;
* improve traceability;
* reduce paper-based processes;
* support evidence-based decision making;
* maintain complete historical records;
* improve communication between office staff and field workers;
* simplify contractor collaboration;
* generate consistent operational documentation;
* provide managers with meaningful operational insights.

---

# 6. Scope

InfraTrack focuses on operational asset management.

The initial product scope includes:

* Asset Management
* Operational activities initiated by business events.
* Field Inspections
* Issue Management
* Work Orders
* Preventive Maintenance
* Corrective Maintenance
* Contractor Management
* Field Reporting
* Document Generation
* Photo Management
* Notifications
* Operational Dashboards
* Audit History

---

# 7. Out of Scope

The following business domains are intentionally excluded from the first release:

* Financial Accounting
* Payroll
* Procurement Management
* Inventory Management
* Human Resources
* Geographic Information System Editing
* Budget Management
* Fleet Management
* Customer Relationship Management

These domains may integrate with InfraTrack but are not part of its core responsibilities.

---

# 8. Success Criteria

InfraTrack will be considered successful when it enables councils to:

* manage the complete lifecycle of operational asset activities from a single platform;
* retrieve the complete history of any asset within seconds;
* produce consistent operational documentation;
* improve visibility of ongoing field operations;
* reduce administrative overhead;
* maintain complete evidence supporting operational decisions;
* simplify collaboration between office staff, field employees and contractors.

---

# 9. Guiding Principles

The following principles will guide every design decision throughout the project.

## Business First

Business processes define the software.

Technology never dictates business behaviour.

## Single Source of Truth

Operational information must exist in one authoritative system.

## Evidence Over Assumptions

Every significant operational action should generate evidence supporting future audits and historical analysis.

## Traceability

Every important decision should be attributable to a person, a date and a business reason.

## Simplicity

The platform should remain simple to understand while supporting complex operational workflows.

Complex business domains should be represented through clear workflows rather than unnecessary technical complexity.

---

# 10. Project Philosophy

InfraTrack is designed as a realistic enterprise software product rather than a technology demonstration.

Every functional decision should be justified by a genuine operational requirement commonly found within Australian local governments.

The objective is not to maximise the number of features, but to faithfully model the operational reality of public asset management.
